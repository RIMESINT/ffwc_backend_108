Introduction

The Flood Forecasting & Warning Centre (FFWC) API provides real-time and historical flood-related data for various river points across Bangladesh. Developed by the Bangladesh Water Development Board (BWDB), this service aims to empower developers, researchers, and public with critical information to enhance disaster preparedness and response.

Our mission is to provide accurate and timely flood information, contributing to the safety and resilience of communities nationwide. This API is designed for easy integration into diverse applications, dashboards, and analytical tools.

Key Features

  • Real-time Water Levels: Access current water levels at designated river stations.
  • Flood Warnings: Receive immediate alerts and warnings for impending flood situations.
  • Forecasts: Get short-term flood forecasts (24-hour, 48-hour) for critical points.
  • Historical Data: Retrieve past water level readings and flood events for analysis.
  • Geospatial Information: Data includes precise location details for each river station.
